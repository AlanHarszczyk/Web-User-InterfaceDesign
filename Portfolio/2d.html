Backstory

This project represents my final work for an animation class. We had to come up with our own character, design concept art for it, and create three animations: idle, walk, and run. We also added different looks for the character. I chose to make a fun, old-school cartoon robot and gave it costumes like pirate, vampire, or prehistoric themes. It's kind of like SpongeBob in style, you know, simple and playful. I used Photoshop to create all the designs and concept art.
